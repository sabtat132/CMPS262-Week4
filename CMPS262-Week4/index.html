Sabrina Tatalias

Hi, my name is Sabrina Tatalias, and I am currently a 1st semester junior at Point Park University - graduating in December 2021!

A little about myself is that I am a Rowland School of Business Ambassador, President of ITSO, and in the Honors Program at Point Park University.

When I'm not on campus, you can find me working or volunteering. I'm on the board of a nonprofit in my community called the Bethel Park Community Foundation. On that board, I oversee our Marketing and PR efforts by overseeing the events, Facebook, website, and internship program.

Since I came to Point Park University, I have been working as a Customer Care Rep at a local start-up in Pittsburgh. Starting February, I will be starting an internship at a marketing firm called Bounteous as a Marketing & Web Analytics Intern. Then, in the summer, I will be a Data Analyst intern at Gateway Health for their Data Services team working for the Data Management and Integration group.
